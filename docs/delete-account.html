Delete Your Account

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within The Day To app.

1
Open the app Launch The Day To on your device
2
Go to Account Tap the menu icon (three dots) and select "Account"
3
Delete Account Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
4
Confirm deletion Review the warning and confirm to permanently delete your account
Warning:

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your journal entries, mood colors, and account data will be permanently removed.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • All journal entries stored in the cloud
  • All custom mood colors stored in the cloud
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • All local data on your device
