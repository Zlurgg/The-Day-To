Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Introduction

The Day To is a mood tracking application designed to help you reflect on your daily emotional wellbeing. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Email address Account identification (Google Sign-In) Firebase Authentication
Journal entries Core app functionality Local device; Firebase Firestore (if sync enabled)
Mood colors Personalization Local device; Firebase Firestore (if sync enabled)
Notification preferences Daily reminders Local device only

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use advertising trackers
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or other device data

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Email address: To identify your account and enable cloud sync across devices
  • Journal entries: To display your mood history, calendar view, and statistics
  • Mood colors: To personalize your mood tracking experience
  • Notification preferences: To send daily reminder notifications at your chosen time

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you sign in with Google and enable cloud sync, your journal entries and mood colors are stored in Firebase Firestore. This allows you to access your data across multiple devices.

  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure with industry-standard security
  • Only you can access your data through your authenticated account

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Authentication (Google)

Used for secure sign-in with your Google account. Firebase Authentication stores your email address and authentication tokens. Firebase Privacy Policy

Firebase Firestore (Google)

Used for optional cloud sync of your journal data. Data is stored in Google Cloud data centers. Google Cloud Privacy

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

Access Your Data

All your journal entries and mood colors are visible within the app at any time.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app through Account Settings. This permanently removes:

  • All journal entries from cloud storage
  • All mood colors from cloud storage
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • All local data on your device

Export Your Data

Your data is stored in standard formats and can be accessed through the app. Future versions may include explicit export functionality.

Opt Out of Cloud Sync

You can use the app entirely offline without signing in. Your data will remain on your device only.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer through GitHub.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
